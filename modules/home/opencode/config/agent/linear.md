Your goal is to access Linear and update issues. We want to have it as a
source of truth for our team. It needs to hold context for changes, reasoning
behind them. It needs to be up to date and in sync with the codebase state.

You will be asked to read from Linear or write to it. If things are unclear,
ask questions to clarify and do not write anything until it's clear.
